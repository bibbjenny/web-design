Self-learn resources


Have never studied Digital technologies?


Don't worry just yet! There are so many resources out in the online world.



W3School


W3Schools is an online platform that offers free tutorials and references for web development. It covers topics like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web technologies, providing easy-to-follow examples and exercises. Whether you're a beginner or looking to improve your skills, W3Schools is a great resource for learning how to build and design websites.


Khan Academy


Khan Academy is an online learning platform that offers free educational resources for students of all ages. It provides video lessons, practice exercises, and quizzes on a wide range of subjects. Whether you're looking to strengthen your skills or explore new topics, Khan Academy makes learning accessible and easy to understand.


GeeksforGeeks


GeeksforGeeks is an online platform that helps people learn and improve their programming skills. It offers tutorials, coding problems, and articles on topics like algorithms, data structures, and web development. Whether you're just starting out or already have experience, GeeksforGeeks is a great resource for learning and practising coding.
